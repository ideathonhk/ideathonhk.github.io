Overview

Ideathon aims to help you create. It's where you can really meet new people and really learn new things. In an Ideathon, You will have an experience similar to founding a startup.

All kinds of ideas are welcome, and all people who are willing to learn and create are welcome! Our mentors will help you go through the whole part. Join us today!

See How It Works

Mentors

Our mentors are all professors and founders of startups. They are experienced in how to make things happen.

If you just want to have fun, our mentors will be the most important reason for you to come. They are talented, and talking with them will be of great joy! They will also be happy if you could learn something from them!

Ideas, Teams and Prototypes

Ideas are an important part of an Ideathon. Teams are formed based on several ideas. Also, prototypes will be built on that idea. Ideas are encouraged to be changed whatever the reason. It's OK if you don't have an idea before the event.

Build a prototype with your team. A prototype could be some drawings, some surveys. It could also be a website, a program, a plan and a real product. You just need to build something during the event. That's the key point of our event.

See How to Prototype

Hard Work and Winner Prize

We welcome people who just want to have fun, as well as people who want to win a prize! Make your presentation in the end incredible, attract the mentors and you will be the winner! In spite of great prize, our winner will be in a program called IdeathonHK Accelerator. Our mentors will further guide your team to really make your prototype usable, and your ideas real.
